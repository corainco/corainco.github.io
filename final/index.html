The Viewing
Pattern Quiz

What shapes the films we watch? Our choices feel personal, but they're influenced by algorithms, marketing, critical consensus, and decades of industry gatekeeping. This assessment examines your film knowledge—which directors you know, which stories you're drawn to, which genres you take seriously, and how you engage with cinema as culture.

By the end, you'll see patterns in your viewing habits you may not have noticed—patterns that often reflect broader industry imbalances rather than personal choices. No judgment, just a mirror. And maybe some surprises about where your knowledge is concentrated and where it has gaps.
